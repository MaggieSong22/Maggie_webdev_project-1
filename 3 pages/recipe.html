Ingredients

  • 2 eggplants
  • 3 zucchinis
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 bell peppers
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper, and herbs

Steps to Cook

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Slice vegetables evenly and arrange in a circular pattern in a baking dish.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes until vegetables are tender.
